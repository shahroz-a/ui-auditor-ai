# Code Of Conduct

This project should be a calm, useful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact differs from intent.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Make room for new contributors to ask questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for focus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or block participants when needed to protect the community. Report concerns privately through the security contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
